About Our Project

Blood Aid is a research-based platform with the goal of creating a Location-Aware Peer-to-Peer Blood Donation System for Bangladesh.

Our platform helps connect blood donors and recipients in real-time, making the process of finding life-saving blood faster and more efficient.

  • Real-time donor tracking with location awareness
  • AI Chatbot Assistant for 24/7 support
  • Secure user data management and privacy protection
  • 3-month rest period system for donor health
