Terms & Conditions
1. Donor Eligibility

You must be between 18-60 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health to donate blood.

2. Privacy Policy

Your personal information will be kept confidential and only shared with blood recipients.

3. Rest Period

After donating blood, you must wait 3 months before donating again for your health and safety.

4. Code of Conduct

Treat all users with respect. Misuse of the platform may result in account suspension.

5. Emergency Contact

Keep your contact information updated for emergency situations.
